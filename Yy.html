The best rock band in the world

Queen is a British rock band formed in London in 1970. The classic lineup consisted of Freddie Mercury (vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar, vocals), Roger Taylor (drums, vocals), and John Deacon (bass). Known for their diverse musical style, elaborate vocal harmonies, and theatrical live performances, Queen became one of the most iconic and influential bands in rock history.
